Autosport International

15 - 16 January 2022

Autosport International, Europe’s largest motorsport show is back!

See the show doors open to racing enthusiasts, race license holders, families and some of motorsport’s biggest celebrities! Fans of all ages will get up close and personal to legendary cars and racing drivers spanning over F1, BTCC, WRC and more fan favourite racing series. All Saturday & Sunday tickets include access to the adrenaline-spiking Live Action Arena show. With gravity-defying stunts, celebrity appearances, the Live Action Arena is 45 minutes of engine-growling, tyre smoking fun.
